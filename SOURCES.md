## Source Notes

This bibliography includes peer-reviewed scientific publications, government reports, technical standards, books, and media coverage related to the Taos Hum phenomenon. Sources span multiple disciplines including acoustics, audiology, geophysics, environmental health, and psychology.

Many early sources (particularly government reports from the 1990s) are now archived and may not be readily available online. University libraries and government document repositories may provide access.

For ongoing research, consult:
- The World Hum Map and Database Project for current citizen science data
- Acoustic journals for latest research on low-frequency sound
- Audiology journals for updates on hearing science
- Environmental health publications for policy developments
